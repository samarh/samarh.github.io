I'm a researcher ath the Center for Language Engineering in Lahore, Pakistan, where I work on building natural language processing tools for Urdu, Pakistan's national language. My research interest are machine learning and its applications. While I work on natural language processing now, in the past I have also employed machine learning for computer vision tasks.

I recieved my Bachelor's in Computer Science from the University of Engineering and Technology, Lahore, in 2016. My senior thesis was on using deep convolutional neural for learning representations from faces, with a focus on predicting facial keypoints.
