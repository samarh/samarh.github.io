I'm a researcher at the Center for Language Engineering in Lahore, Pakistan, where I work on the natural language processing of low-resource, regional languages, with a focus on Urdu -- Pakistan's national langauge. My research interests centre around machine learning and its applications. Recent recent has involved multilingual language identification, extractive text summarization, sentiment analysis, and content filtering.

I recieved my Bachelor's in Computer Science from the University of Engineering and Technology, Lahore, in 2016. My senior thesis was on the application of deep convolutional neural networks for learning representations from faces in order to predict facial keypoints. Other academic projects include a vehicle license plate recognition system, a handwritten digit recognizer, and a study of the patterns underlying soccer statistics using data mining techniques.

In my spare time I enjoy reading and learning new stuff. I am an avid table tennis and soccer player.
